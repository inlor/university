Welcome

Parsons is a French online learning platform aimed at professional adults and students, developed in May 2010. As of Jan 2020, the platform has more than 50 million students and 57,000 instructors teaching courses in over 65 languages. There have been over 295 million course enrollments.

 How does it work ? Please read !

The platform contains 2 general usages: for teachers and for students.

Here are some of the basics you need to know in order to use our tool as teacher.

To indent your code, you need to insert 2 spaces. This means that 2 spaces == 1 indent.

Your solution need to be writen in the correct order. To use distractors, you need to add #distractor at the end of a line.
